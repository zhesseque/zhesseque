About Pavlodar

Pavlodar is a city located in northeastern Kazakhstan.
As of 2010, the city had a population of 331,710.
The population of Pavlodar mainly consist of Kazakhs, Russians and Ukrainian, German and Tatar minorities

Famous personalities come from Pavlodar:

  1. Shaken Kenzhetaiuly Aimanov - a well-known Kazakh actor and director. People's artist of the USSR since 1964. He was born and raised in Bayanaul District of Pavlodar region.
  2. Satpayev Kanysh Imantayevich - geologist, doctor of mineralogical sciences, professor, academician. The first academician of Kazakh origin.
  3. Sultanmahmut Toraygirov- Kazakh poet, educator-Democrat
  4. Zhussipbek Aymauytov - an outstanding Kazakh writer, playwright, publicist, one of the founders of Kazakh literature.
